ADEX LEGENDS

Fight For Your Existence

Description

Adex Legends is a battle free-to-play shooting game. We launched Adex Legends in 2019 and now has reached over 1 million downloads and over 500 thousand players are online everyday. Adex Legends won the most popular shooting game of 2020-2021 (on Google Play Store). Come on join the fun! Available in PC, iOS and Android.

Game Mode

Arena

Adex Legends currently have 4 arenas. Those map names are Drop-Off, Encore, Habitat, and Party Crasher. Reach level 4 to enter your first arena, Party Crasher.

Battle Royale

There are currently 4 battle royales of Adex Legends. Those are King's Canyon, World Edge, Olympus, and Storm Point. Players need to reach level 2 to enter King's Canyon and World Edge.

Overview

Level up your legends, open up more maps! For every game round won, players will get free in-game money to enhance their weapons. Time-limited events are also a way for players to reach the Adex Legends mastery. To unlock more game features of Adex Legends, players may buy the seasonal Battle Pass, rewarding them with Adex Coins and various unique items which aren't purchasable using regular free in-game currency. Adex Legends will hold an international offline e-sports competition for professional teams.
